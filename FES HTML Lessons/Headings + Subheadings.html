Top 3 Places in Austin - A Guide for the Solo Traveler

1. Texas Capital

The Austin Capital is a must see, there is nothing more important than digging into the roots and history of this lively city.
Purchase your tickets online beforhand for easy access

2. Barton Springs

A famous local attraction near the city center that brings together the community during the roasting Texas summer.
Be sure to bring a cool beer and some pals!

3. Elephant Room

An underground quaint jazz bar where you'll have the opportunity to mingle with like-minded individuals from all over the city.
They have great drinks too!
